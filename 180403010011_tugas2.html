Meet Uzumaki Naruto

CREW :

    Naruto is a series of manga by Masashi Kishimoto which was adapted into an anime series . Naruto's manga tells the story of the life of the main character, Naruto Uzumaki , a hyperactive, cheerful, and ambitious ninja who wants to realize his desire to get the title of Hokage , the strongest leader and ninja in his village. The series is based on a one-shot comic by Kishimoto which was published in the edition of Akamaru Jump in August 1997.
